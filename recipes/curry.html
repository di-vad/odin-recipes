Curry Udon

Udon served with a Curry sauce

This japanese dish combines the nation's two favourite foods into one.
Follow along this recipe to make and prepare this simple, vegetarian dish.

Ingredients:

  • Udon noodles
  • Curry powder, 1/2 cup
  • White onions, sliced, half of an onion
  • Green Onions, to garnish

Steps:

  1. Boil the noodles, until they are cooked.
  2. In a Seperate pot, saute the onions until caramelized.
  3. Add the curry powder, and cook out the roux.
  4. Add 1/2 cup of water, and add in the cooked udon noodles.
  5. Cook until well-combined, finish with green onions.
