Candied Anchovies

Korean-style Anchovies stir-fried and served as a side

This sweet side dish is a great and easy way to incorporate anchovies into your diet!
15-min is all you need to make and prepare this "banchan" staple.

Ingredients:

  • Dried anchovies, 1 cup
  • Granulated sugar, 1 Tbsp
  • Soy sauce, 1 Tbsp
  • Syrup, 1 Tbsp
  • Toasted sesame seeds, 1 Tsp
  • Sesame seed oil, 1 Tbsp

Steps:

  1. Toast the dried anchovies in a pan, without any oil. Toast for around 5 minutes.
  2. Prepare the marinade sauce:
    1. Combine the sugar, soy sauce, and the syrup in a bowl
    2. Mix well, until homogenous.
  3. Add in oil to the pan, frying the anchovies until the desired texture is reached.
  4. Gather the fried anchovies to the side, and cook off the marinade in the open spot.
  5. Once the marinade has been cooked out and is bubbling, combine with the anchovies.
  6. Mix and toss well to incorporate the sauce into the anchovies.
  7. Add the sesame seed oil and seeds, and toss yet again to mix.
  8. Remove from pan, and let cool. Serve room-temperature.
